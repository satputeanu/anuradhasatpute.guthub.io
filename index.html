About Me

I am an experienced Employee Engagement Specialist, with a proven track record of designing and implementing employee feedback programmes and advising large global and diverse organisations on driving culture and engagement change to achieve positive impact on key metrics.


I design and manage large, complex employee engagement programmes across geographies, varied businesses and diverse cultures to reverse attrition and manage business change. I analyse data to develop insights for senior business leaders to make informed decisions, define success criteria and build dashboards to track success. I am highly experienced in managing multi-stranded projects with cross functional teams and accommodating rapid changes to deliver tight timelines and planned success criteria. Furthermore, I coach and train managers, HR and leaders to enable positive behaviour change.

Strategy + Engagment = Performance

This is what I have focused on for most of my career: ✓ Employee Engagement ✓ Stakeholder Management ✓ People Analytics (SPSS & Tableau) ✓ Surveys (Employee & Customer) ✓ Training – Designing & Delivery ✓ Mentoring & Coaching – Strengths Finder ✓ Quantitative & Qualitative Research ✓ Programme Management ✓ Project Management ✓ Change Management ✓ Leadership Development ✓ Organisational Development ✓ Data Analysis ✓ Financial Services ✓ New Tech ✓ Diversity & Inclusion (D&I) .
